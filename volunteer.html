Lizzie Biddle

Lizzie Biddle holds a Master of Arts in Peace and Conflict Studies from the UNC Greensboro. She worked with immigrants and refugees in Greensboro at the University of North Carolina Greensboro’s Center for New North Carolinians for ten years. Throughout her time at the CNNC, She spent the bulk of her time overseeing services and programs in four different community-based sites in area neighborhoods with large numbers of newcomers. As part of her studies, she completed a graduate student internship with the U.S. State Department Bureau of Population, Refugees and Migration in Washington DC, Lizzie has traveled extensively internationally with the specific goal of studying forced migration, Lizzie now works as a trainer and organizer with the Racial Equity Institute.
